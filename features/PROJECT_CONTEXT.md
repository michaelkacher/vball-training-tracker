# Project Context

**What we're building:** A volleyball training tracker that helps athletes plan and track their workout programs with categorized exercises.

**Primary users:** Volleyball athletes (players and coaches) who want to build structured training plans focused on sport-specific skills and fitness.

**Key goal:** Makes it easy for athletes to create customized workout plans from pre-defined volleyball-specific categories, track their progress, and maintain training consistency.

**Tech stack:** Hono (backend), Fresh + Preact (frontend), Deno KV (database)
